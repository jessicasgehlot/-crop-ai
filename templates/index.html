Crop Variety Guide — What to grow, when & how
Crop Disease Awareness
Wheat Rust
Yellow/brown pustules on leaves. Use resistant varieties & fungicide spray.
Rice Blast
Diamond-shaped lesions on leaves. Apply Tricyclazole fungicide early.
Cotton Bollworm
Larvae damage bolls. Use Bt cotton varieties & pheromone traps.
Maize Blight
Grayish lesions on leaves. Rotate crops & apply Mancozeb.
Sugarcane Smut
Black whip-like growth. Use disease-free setts & hot water treatment.
Vegetable Blight
Water-soaked lesions. Improve drainage & use copper-based fungicides.

How the AI Works
1
Data Collection
Farmer enters 8 soil & weather parameters into the system.
2
Preprocessing
Data is normalized using StandardScaler for fair comparison.
3
K-Means Clustering
Input is assigned to the nearest cluster of 9 crop groups.
4
Recommendation
Best crop + fertilizer, irrigation & seasonal advice is shown.
